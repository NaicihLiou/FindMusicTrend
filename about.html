    Music is a key element in people's life. It links people in the world together.
    Knowing the trend of music brings people more common language and connections.
    Our project aims to show the features of top songs on Spotify during 2010 to 2019 based from data perspectives.
    We provide searching service by the order of singer name, published year, and song gernes.
    Moreover, the network graph of the collaborational relationship between singers is shown to reveal more interesting features of top songs.
    Please enjoy the exploration of the trend of music!
